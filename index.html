About Zakat

Zakat is one of the five pillars of Islam. It is a mandatory charitable contribution, usually calculated as 2.5% of qualifying wealth held for a lunar year.

Who Must Pay?

Muslim adults who possess wealth above the Nisab threshold must pay Zakat.

What is Nisab?

The Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth one must have before Zakat becomes due. This calculator uses the silver standard of 612.36 grams.
